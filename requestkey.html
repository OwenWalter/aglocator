Request Author Key

Use this page to obtain your unique update key for editing the entries you have submitted to AG Locator database.

Your author key will be emailed to the address provided shortly after the request is submitted. Be sure to specify the email address used when the entry was created. The current author email address is shown at the top of the page when modifying a signature.

Existing database entries are only able to be updated by the original author (validated by their email address). If you feel that another user's entry needs to be edited, have taken over the role of the former author, or need to perform bulk updates, please contact the team for assistance.
