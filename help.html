Help & Information

Using the Locator

The Assignment Group Locator is a tool to quickly find the appropriate support group for various products and categories. Simply type a keyword, product name, or a known assignment group into the search bar on the main page.

Filtering Results

You can narrow your search by selecting a support category from the "Filter" dropdown next to the search bar. This helps when a keyword is associated with multiple types of issues (e.g., Installation, Configuration).

Managing Entries

Adding a New Entry

To add a new entry, click on the "Add New Entry" link in the top navigation bar. You will be asked to provide details such as the keyword, category, and assignment group. An email address is required to manage the entry later.

Modifying or Deleting an Entry

You can only modify or delete entries that you have created. To do so, you need an Author Key, which is tied to the email address you provided when creating the entry.

  • First, search for your entry on the main page.
  • Click on the entry in the search results to view its details.
  • On the details page, click the "Modify This Entry" link.
  • You will be prompted to enter your Author Key to proceed with edits or deletion.

Requesting an Author Key

If you've lost your Author Key or need one for the first time, you can request one via the "Request Author Key" page. The key will be sent to the email address registered with your entries.

Contact & Support

If you encounter any issues, need to take ownership of an entry from a previous author, or have suggestions for improvement, please reach out.
